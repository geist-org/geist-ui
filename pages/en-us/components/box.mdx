## Box

Polymorphic scalable component.

<Playground

/>

| ...       | scale props  | `ScaleProps`        | `'width', 'px', 'font', ...` | -       |
| ...       | native props | `HTMLAttributes`    | `'id', 'className', ...`     | -       |
